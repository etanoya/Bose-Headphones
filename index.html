Frequently Asked Questions

How does noise cancelling work?
Bose Noise Canceling Headphones 700 uses both active and passive noise reduction technology. They use advanced patented electronics to actively reduce noise by placing microphones both inside and outside of the ear cups. The electronic system detects the sound in almost every environment and measures, compares and then responds continuously - to produce a neutralizing signal. Passive noise reduction is achieved by the combination of the acoustic design and the materials selected for the ear cups and pillows.
Can Bose Noise Canceling Headphones 700 Receive Calls?
Yes. The Bose Noise Canceling Headphones 700 features an adaptive four-microphone system that allows you to use the headphones during calls when connected to any smartphone. In addition to traditional calls, headphones can work with many apps that use Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP), such as Skype or FaceTime.
Can Bose Noise Canceling Headphones 700 be connected to two mobile devices at the same time?
Yes. The multipoint technology enables the Bose Headphones 700 to be connected to two devices at the same time - e.g. an iPad and a smartphone. So while watching a video on the connected iPad, the headphones automatically pause the video to answer a call from the connected smartphone. When the call is completed, the headphones return to the video on the iPad.
How long does the battery last?
Bose Noise Canceling Headphones 700 uses a specially designed rechargeable lithium-ion battery that provides up to 20 hours of full-featured audio playback.
